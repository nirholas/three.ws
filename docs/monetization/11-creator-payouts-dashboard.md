# Prompt 11: Creator Payouts & Dashboard

## Objective
Develop a system for creators to see their earnings and withdraw their funds.

## Explanation
Creators need a clear and transparent way to track their sales and access their earnings. This involves building a creator dashboard and a secure withdrawal process.

## Instructions
1.  **Create a Creator Dashboard Page:**
    *   Design a new page for creators to view their sales data.
    *   Display total earnings, sales per skill, and a list of recent transactions.

2.  **Implement a Payouts API:**
    *   Create a backend process for handling creator withdrawals.
    *   This might involve a smart contract that holds the funds and allows creators to claim their share, or a centralized system that processes payouts periodically.

3.  **Security:**
    *   Ensure the payout process is secure and that only the legitimate creator can withdraw their funds. This might involve connecting their wallet and signing a message to verify ownership.
