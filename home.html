How It Works

From file to deployed agent
in three steps.

No account required to start. Drop a model, explore the viewer, then sign in when you're ready to save and share.

01

Drop your model

Drag a GLB or glTF 2.0 file directly into the browser. Instant WebGL rendering — no upload, no install, no waiting.

02

Animate & configure

Pick animations, add an AI chat agent, adjust lighting and camera. Save your avatar to your account for reuse.

03

Embed anywhere

Generate a widget — one iframe, any site. Turntable, talking agent, animation gallery. Works in Notion, Ghost, WordPress.
