three.ws turns your Seeker into a 3D agent studio.

Capture a selfie, generate a real glTF avatar, and mint it as an on-chain agent owned by your Seed Vault wallet. Every signature happens inside the Seeker's secure element — keys never touch the app layer.

WHAT YOU CAN DO

• Capture & mint. Take three selfies; three.ws reconstructs a textured 3D avatar in seconds and writes it on-chain as a Metaplex Core NFT held by your wallet.
• Discover. Browse 3D agents minted by the community — drag to orbit, tap to inspect, hold to enter a walkaround.
• Customize. Swap accessories, change animations, tune voices, and re-mint the updated metadata.
• Embed anywhere. Every agent has a shareable URL and a one-line embed snippet — drop yours into Telegram, X, or your own site.
• Sign with Seed Vault. Wallet linking, transaction approval, and SIWS (Sign-In with Solana) all route through Mobile Wallet Adapter — no copy-pasting seed phrases, no browser extensions.

WHY SEEKER

Phantom and Solflare on a phone are good. Seed Vault is better. three.ws talks to the Seeker's on-device wallet via the Mobile Wallet Adapter protocol, so signing flows live inside the hardware-isolated TEE. The same agents you mint on Seeker show up automatically on the three.ws web app — your wallet, your library, anywhere you sign in.

OPEN BY DEFAULT

three.ws is open source (github.com/nirholas/three.ws). Agents follow the ERC-8004 / Metaplex Core specifications, so they're portable to any compliant marketplace or wallet. No vendor lock-in.

REQUIREMENTS

• A Solana wallet — Seed Vault on Seeker / Saga, or any standard Solana wallet (Phantom, Solflare, Backpack) when paired with the browser version.
• Camera permission (for selfie capture).
• A small amount of SOL to cover mint fees (typically < 0.01 SOL).
