three.ws · Reputation attestations on Base

A reputation primitive for autonomous agents: read and write on-chain attestations for any address. This demo runs entirely on Base Sepolia (testnet, chainId 84532) and talks directly to the Ethereum Attestation Service contract at 0x4200000000000000000000000000000000000021. No backend, no custodial keys — you sign every write yourself.
