three.ws · Skill royalty distribution

A royalty primitive for skill authors: deploy a single pull-split contract on Base Sepolia (testnet, chainId 84532) that automatically slices every incoming ETH payment by your configured percentages. Fund it once, then call distribute — each author can withdraw their share whenever. No backend, no custodial keys; you sign every write yourself. The contract is immutable (no owner).
