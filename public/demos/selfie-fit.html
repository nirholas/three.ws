Selfie Fit

Three-photo capture with live face-landmark quality gates. Each slot runs a 468-point wireframe overlay and head-pose estimator on the webcam feed (or a single uploaded image) and only unlocks when the yaw lands in the expected range. Submit posts the three JPEGs to /api/onboarding/avaturn-session and opens the returned three.ws Selfie editor session in an iframe. Sandbox only — does not touch the live /create or /avatars/:id flows.
